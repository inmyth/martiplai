Software Development

We have developed a wide range of software from web sites, mobile applications, cloud, IoT, micro-services. However we understand that your business is unique. For that reason we dedicate our effort to understand the challenges you face before we devise the solution. In general we use the following steps in our projects:

Problem Analysis

In general we will discuss your objective and prepare a general set of requirements. We will also advise you of possible area of improvement.

Architecture Design

Once the requirements are set, we will draw up technical plans. From here we will help you choose the best direction based on cost-efficiency, ease of development, and other criteria.

Development

We will carry out the actual implementation during this period. During the phase we will also inform you of the partial result or adjust the direction based on your feedback.

Support, Scaling

We offer support in the form of ticket issues and maintenance for ongoing product operation. We also provide post-delivery development for additional feature.
